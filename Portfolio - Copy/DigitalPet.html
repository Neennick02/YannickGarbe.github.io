Digital Pet

About

Take care of your character to keep him happy.
Feed, sleep, clean and play with him to stay healthy.
Use food collected in the minigame to buy outfits.

Project Info

Engine: Unity
Target Platform: JavaScript Webpage
Development time: 2 weeks
Team size: 1 developer

Introduction.

This digital pet project is inspired by the retro tamagotchi toys.
Collect food from the minigame to feed your player and buy collectables.
When playing for to long the player becomes tired and needs to rest or consume a energy drink to refill his energy bar.
If the player becomes unhealth poop appears in his house and can be cleaned to heal him.

Gameplay

The UI shows player bars that deplete over time, even when quitting the page the progress doesnt get lost.
All bars include:
- Hunger
- Energy
- Health
- Happiness

In the minigame the player has to collect all the falling food by moving from side to side.
Over time the falling speed increases and the player becomes tired.

Food collected in the minigame can be used to buy collectables from the shop
that can be equipped in the closet.

If the player becomes unhealthy poop appears inside the home,
this can be cleaned by clicking it which increases the health stat.

Systems I worked on.

  • Player Movement – Basic arrow key movement in the home and 1 dimensional movement with screenwrap in the minigame

  • Minigame –Falling food that can be collected, speeds up and changes position when reappearing.

  • Status Bars – Bars that deplete over time or based on actions. Drinking energy drink is unhealthy and catching food comsumes energy.

  • Shop – Buy different hats with food and equip items in the closet menu. Automatically shows if an item has been bought or is available to buy.

  • Poop mechanic – Spawns poop in random location inside the home.

  • Sleep mechanic – Turns of the lights, takes away control and refills the enery bar.

  • Settings – Change sound settings and shows controls.

What I've learned.

While using store items function I leaned how I could save all kinds of data like:
–the progression of bars
– player position
– current room
– bought items
– equipped items
– food in the inventory.

This was the first project where I made some of my own assets, I had a lot of fun making the different expressions, UI buttons and clothing peaces.

Working with sound inside the P5.js Librairy was new to me but it didnt take to much time to figure out.

I had some issues with the collision detection in the minigame because I only used the P5.js Librairy.
In this time I wasnt aware of how to use JavaScript buttons so in retrospective I made this a lot harder for myself.

To save on resources I decided to loop the same food and only change the position and speed.

with only 6 weeks of programming experience I was really proud of what I was able to make in 2 weeks with little help
